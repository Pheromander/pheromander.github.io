About The Game

In Pheromander, you are in command of a colony of ant-like agents. Your duty is to help your colony to expand by collecting resources and by defending against enemies. Like real ants, you cannot control them justy by selecting them and sending them to their destination. Instead, you have to use digital pheromones to guide your agents to mineral sources and to coordinate battles.

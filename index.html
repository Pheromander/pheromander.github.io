About The Game

Pheromander is a real-time strategy game about swarms. You are in control of a colony of ant like agent. Your duty is to expand and protedt your colony by collecting resources and defeating enemies. Similar to real ants, you cannot instruct your agents directly. Instead, you have to guide them to mineral sources and to coordinate battles.

Features

Unique controls

Use digital pheromones to command up to 200 units

Two game modes

Simulation: Create custom scenarios by placing resources and enemies on the map

Challenge: Compete against the clock and score as many points as possible by collecting resources

Experimentation

Change the pheromone perception of agents and properties of pheromones to adapt and optimize your commands to the current situation
